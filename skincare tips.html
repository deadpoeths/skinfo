skincare tips to keep in mind


1. Apply sunscreen every day before you go outside.
Sunscreen is the closest thing we have to the fountain of youth.
It can really slow down skin aging. It may also help prevent skin cancer.
Look for a sunscreen that offers broad-spectrum protection,
SPF 30 (or higher), and is water-resistant

2. Practice gentle cleansing Gentle cleansing helps skin
look its best. Moisten it with lukewarm water to gently cleanse your face.
Then apply a gentle cleanser, gently applying it in a circular motion with
your fingertips. Finish by rinsing off the cleanser completely and gently
patting your face dry with a clean towel. Also remember to remove makeup before bed.

3. Never forget to do a patch test You should always do a patch test before
using a new product to avoid the risk of irritation or an allergic reaction.
Just apply a small amount of the product to the inner part of the arm to see
how your skin reacts to the formula.
